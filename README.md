SmartSeestar is a comprehensive deep space imaging planner tailored for users of Seestar smartscopes (S50, S30, S30 Pro). It empowers Seestar astrophotographers to effortlessly plan, track, and manage their imaging sessions by providing real-time visibility data for celestial objects like nebulae, galaxies, and star clusters based on their location and sky direction. The app integrates essential tools such as detailed weather forecasts, atmospheric conditions, light pollution maps, and an exposure calculator to optimize imaging plans. Users can also maintain a personalized wishlist of targets, log their imaging sessions, and explore a rich deep-space catalog to enhance their astrophotography experience and capture stunning images.
